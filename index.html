ABOUT US

RESEARCHERS @ THE HCII

As adults-in-training ourselves, we wanted to try and find a way to leverage technology to ease the awkward transition into adulthood.
Adulting101 is a human-centered approach of learning many of the things that are important to being a capable and self-reliant adult – and an attempt to make the adoption of these habits (in all of their mundane glory) a little easier.
